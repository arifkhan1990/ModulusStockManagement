Features & Functionalities
1. Multi-Channel Stock Management
✅ Real-Time Inventory Tracking – Monitor stock levels across multiple locations, stores, and warehouses.
✅ Multi-Warehouse & Multi-Store Support – Manage stock movements between different warehouses or retail locations.
✅ Multi-Supplier & Multi-Customer Support – Maintain detailed supplier and customer records for seamless transactions.
✅ Barcode & QR Code Scanning – Enable fast and accurate stock updates using barcode scanners or mobile devices.
✅ Stock Adjustments & Transfers – Adjust stock for damages, returns, or internal transfers.

2. Automated Inventory Control & Optimization
✅ Low Stock Alerts & Reorder Notifications – Set automatic notifications when stock reaches a minimum threshold.
✅ Automated Purchase Order Generation – Auto-generate purchase orders based on inventory forecasts.
✅ Batch & Expiry Date Tracking – Keep track of product batches and expiration dates, especially for perishable goods.
✅ Lot & Serial Number Tracking – Maintain detailed tracking for serialized inventory and traceability compliance.

3. Advanced Reporting & Analytics
✅ Real-Time Dashboard – Gain instant insights into stock levels, sales trends, and financial performance.
✅ Customizable Reports – Generate detailed reports on stock valuation, turnover rates, and shrinkage.
✅ AI-Powered Demand Forecasting – Leverage machine learning for predicting stock demand and optimizing restocking.
✅ ABC Inventory Analysis – Categorize stock into high-value, moderate-value, and low-value items for better management.

4. Seamless Order & Supplier Management
✅ Purchase Order Management – Create, track, and receive purchase orders from multiple suppliers.
✅ Sales Order & Invoice Management – Streamline the sales process with automated invoicing and order processing.
✅ Dropshipping & Supplier Integration – Enable direct fulfillment from suppliers without maintaining inventory.
✅ Vendor & Supplier Rating System – Evaluate supplier performance based on order accuracy, delivery time, and quality.

5. User Roles & Access Control
✅ Multi-User Access with Role-Based Permissions – Assign different access levels to employees based on responsibilities.
✅ Audit Trail & Activity Logs – Track every action taken by users to ensure security and compliance.

6. Integrations & API Support
✅ E-commerce Integration (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, etc.) – Sync stock levels and orders automatically.
✅ Accounting Software Integration (QuickBooks, Xero, Zoho Books) – Automate financial tracking and reporting.
✅ Shipping & Logistics Integration (FedEx, UPS, DHL) – Streamline order fulfillment and shipping management.
✅ Third-Party API Access – Extend functionality with API access for custom integrations.

7. Multi-Language & Multi-Currency Support
✅ Global Language Support – Translate the platform into multiple languages for international operations.
✅ Multi-Currency & Tax Support – Automatically convert currencies and apply region-specific tax rules.

8. Mobile & Cloud-Based Access
✅ Progressive Web App (PWA) & Mobile Optimization – Access your stock data from any device, anywhere.
✅ Offline Mode – Work without an internet connection, syncing data when online.

9. Security & Compliance
✅ End-to-End Encryption & Secure Authentication – Protect sensitive data with advanced security measures.
✅ GDPR & HIPAA Compliance – Ensure regulatory compliance for data protection and privacy.
✅ Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) – Strengthen login security for users.

10. Subscription Plans & Pricing
✅ Flexible Pricing Models – Choose from monthly, annual, or enterprise pricing plans.
✅ Free Trial & Demo Signup – Experience the full functionality of MSM before committing.
✅ Custom Enterprise Solutions – Tailor MSM to fit your large-scale business needs.
